About Us


The idea of ​​creating the Grow company was laid in 2018,
developing and producing high-quality and absolutely safe
balanced nutrition, health and beauty products that meet all
international production and food safety standards.

In 2023, an expert group led by a successful businessman Altyn Dastanovich Dastanov decided to create an international company Grow, where everyone gets access to high-quality products and the opportunity to realize themselves financially to the maximum.

And network marketing was chosen as the main method of promotion, as the main trend in sales around the world. We took into account all the disadvantages of many companies in this area and provided a ready-made business solution with a profitable reward system for its partners, which will allow each partner to feel the delights and advantages of network business from the very first steps.

Our goal is to create such conditions in Grow in which everyone will feel safe, will be able to reveal their potential and achieve their goals.

Grow products have their own modern production in Turkey. This allows us to influence product quality control, develop and launch new products, control pricing and make pleasant promotions for our partners.

We produce organic products without the use of synthetic components. The main product areas are: functional nutrition, dietary supplements, vitamins, hygiene products and cosmetics. The recipe of our products is developed on the basis of natural substances, the main one of which is high-quality black cumin oil (lat. Nigella sativa, turk. Çörek otu), which is beneficial for our body. The quality of the products is confirmed by clinical trials and certificates of conformity (GMP, Halal, EAC, HACCP). The re-consumption of products allows us to create a good turnover in the structures of our partners and, accordingly, excellent remuneration.

Founder of Grow

Altyn Dastanovich Dastanov, President of Grow, is originally from Kyrgyzstan and takes pride in his roots, which drive him to explore new opportunities and build successful businesses.

As an experienced entrepreneur, his business interests span Kyrgyzstan, Russia, and Turkey, reflecting his international vision. His journey is one of hard work, perseverance, and a relentless pursuit of growth. With each new project, he demonstrates that starting from scratch doesn’t mean limitations—dreams can be achieved.

Altyn Dastanovich believes in teamwork and cooperation, leading by example. He inspires us to reach global heights while staying true to our values. Discover our products and join Grow to become part of our success story.

With us you can achieve your goals and make your dreams come true!

To make network marketing accessible and profitable for the maximum number of people who want to change their lives for the better.
